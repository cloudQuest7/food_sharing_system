Share more, waste less

We make it easy to share your extra food with your community. Whether you're an individual, a business, or a nonprofit, we have a solution for you.

Post Food

In just a few taps, let us know what you have to share. We'll help you find a local organization that can put it to good use.

Claim Food

Find food near you that's available for pickup. You can claim as much or as little as you need.

Deliver Food

Help get food from donors to recipients. You can deliver whenever and wherever works for you.

Who can use FoodShare?

Our platform is designed to help individuals, businesses, and nonprofits share food with their communities. Here's how it works for each group:

For individuals

Got extra food? Share it with neighbors in need.

For businesses

Restaurants, grocery stores, and other businesses can use FoodShare to donate surplus food to local organizations.

For nonprofits

Local organizations can use FoodShare to receive donations and connect with volunteers who can help distribute food.
